## Important Notes

1. **Always check usage rights** - Make sure you have permission to use these logos
2. **Follow brand guidelines** - Each company has specific rules about logo usage
3. **Use official sources** - Download from official brand guidelines pages only
4. **Respect trademarks** - These are trademarked logos, use them appropriately

## Quick Download Tips

- Most companies provide logos in multiple formats (PNG, SVG, JPG)
- Prefer PNG with transparent backgrounds
- Use the horizontal/landscape versions when available
- Ensure logos are high resolution (at least 200px width)
